A bit about me!

Heyo! I go about with my online name being Nived, I came up with this name back in 2013 when making my first Minecraft account. I ended up going with my real name backwards (very original, I know :P). Ever since then, the name has stuck with me for good!

My hobbies consist of Skating, Playing guitar, and Digital art (duhh). next to these hobbies, I study software development!

My art experience!

I've never gone to a dedicated school or college for anything art related, other than arts n' crafts in high school. Yet I've been at it for just about 4 years now, learning all I can in my free time!

I've posted a lot of older art onto my twitter page, feel free to check it out! :D
